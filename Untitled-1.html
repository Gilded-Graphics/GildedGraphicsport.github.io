My Specialties

I focus on clear visual systems and production-ready assets across brand, packaging, and motion. Here’s where I’m strongest:

Adobe Illustrator

Illustration, vector systems, iconography, and logo craft — strong brand discipline and scalability.

Adobe Photoshop

Photo manipulation, advanced cutouts, compositing, non-destructive workflow, and polish.

Adobe InDesign

Presentation design and editorial layout with strong grid, type hierarchy, and print prep.

3D & Motion

Blender & Adobe Dimension materials/lighting; motion & video editing fundamentals.

About Me

I’m a South Africa–based graphic designer focused on clear, memorable brand systems. I blend strategy with craft across logos, visual identities, packaging, and motion graphics.

  • Services: Branding & Identity, Packaging, Editorial, Social, Motion
  • Toolbox: Adobe CC (AI, PS, ID, AE, PR), Figma, Blender
  • Approach: research-driven concepts, typographic rigor, color systems
